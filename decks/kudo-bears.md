# Kudo Bears

**Commander:** Kudo, King Among Bears
**Theme:** Bear tribal tokens and buffs

## Game Plan
- Curve out early with low-cost bears and mana dorks.
- Deploy anthem effects to turn small bears into serious threats.
- Use token makers and protection spells to rebuild after sweepers.
